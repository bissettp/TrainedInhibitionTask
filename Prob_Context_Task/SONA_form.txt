
Study Name: << Decision Making Study >>

Abstract: Complete a cognitive task (~1.5 hours) and receive $15-$20.


Description: Help us learn more about how people make decisions! The Poldrack lab is 
recruiting healthy adults to participate in a decision making study. Participants will 
come into the lab to complete a computer task (around 1.5 hours) and receive $15 + a bonus
of up to $5 based on how well they do. Please sign up for an open slot or email
ieisenbe@stanford.edu if you have any questions.
